Block Making Machine

We hereby provide you a block / bricks making machine working on our latest developments in the field of hydraulics and pneumatics. Our block making machine provides you the best system for manufacturing of different types of blocks including the fly ash blocks, paver blocks and concrete blocks, manufactured as per IS 15658 : 2006 standards. Our block making machines work on the successfully developed and analyzed vibration technique, multiple vibrations during feeding and casting of the blocks is the prime feature which ensures even distribution of pressure applied from the top to give you the best quality outputs. The technique integrated by us for the manufacturing of the blocks ensures manufacturing of good quality blocks and maintaining the consistency of performance of the machine.

Advantages

  • Well balanced and steady hydraulic mechanism.

  • Working on successfully tested and implemented vibro-compression technology.

  • High productivity.

  • Producing dimensionally accurate blocks.

  • Low Maintenance

  • Easy to operate.

  • Heavy duty and robust structure.

Block Making Machine
